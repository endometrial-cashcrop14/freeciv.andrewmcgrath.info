## 🧭 How Longturn works

Longturn is a slower style of Freeciv play.

- Each turn lasts about 23 hours
- Players log in once a day
- You can make your moves in one session
- Click Turn Done when you finish
- The next turn starts after all players finish or the timer ends

This makes it easier to play a full strategy game without staying online for long periods.
